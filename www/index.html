About this project:

campaigCON is the product of a two day hackathon to end iCorruption (Institutional Corruption).

This project focused on the relationship between campaign contributions and congressional candidates using FEC data to explore both the short term and long term trends of money in politics. The site is currently under futher development. Please check back for updates and more features for exploring this crucial information.
